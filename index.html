about woku
QWhat is woku?
A small private community group chat on Telegram. Not public, but open to anyone who wants to join.
QWhat do you do in woku?
The group is centered around Japanese content — anime, hentai, music. Content can be quite mature since there are no rules, so tread accordingly.
QHow long have you been around?
Started in August 2017 as a hentai group chat on Facebook Messenger. The legacy of that beginning is still very much alive today.
QHow do I join?
Through Telegram. The group is named woqhentai. Hit the button below — there is a small ₱1 entry fee paid via Cinnabank.
QWhat is the deal with "woq"?
It used to refer to "woke" but that meaning has long been discarded. The current name has no meaning. woku is just woq in Japanese — for the katakana aesthetic.
QWhat is woqanime?
A small anime streaming project on Telegram run by us. Stream and download on mobile or desktop — free for woku members.
QWhy so many woqs?
woq is the name of the group as a whole, sometimes called woku or woqhentai. woqanime is the Telegram anime project. woqsukebei is its less active hentai counterpart. The Telegram group chat is woqhentai. Just join the Telegram group — that is where everything happens.
